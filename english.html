I received a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Karabuk University. I have been working as a Research Assistant at Zonguldak Bülent Ecevit University since February 2022. Our project titled Analysis of Panoramic Dental Images with Deep Learning Methods, which we developed with my supervisor, received support within the scope of 2209-A University Students Research Projects Support Program. I am pursuing my Master's Degree in Computer Engineering.
